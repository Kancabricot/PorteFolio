Core Defenders is a 2-player local co-op game with tower defense elements. It was a school project with the goal of making it all the way to a Switch release — although unfortunately, that didn't happen. Players control robots called "Clics" who use slime to defend themselves against the "Klangs," a faction of robots that have enslaved the Clics.

Our main gameplay inspirations were *Overcooked* and *Bish Bash Bots*.

We won the "Grand Prix Jeu Vidéo 2024" from ESMA!!!
